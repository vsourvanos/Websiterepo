PILATES

WHAT IS PILATES?

Pilates is a type of exercise and body conditioning first used as a way for dancers to recover from injury. Today, people other than dancers do Pilates for its health benefits. It highlights precise movements, controlled breathing, and muscle engagement, enhancing muscle tone and stability.

BENEFITS ▼

  • Builds muscle strength
  • Flexibility
  • Better posture
  • Controlled breathing

THE TYPE OF WORKOUTS WE PROVIDE:

CLASSIC PILATES
- focuses on controlled movements, proper breathing, and precise technique performed in a structured sequence. It helps improve core strength, posture and overall body awareness while supporting balance and injury prevention.

MAT WORKOUTS
- especially good for building core strength, improving flexibility, enhancing control, and offering a simple, accessible workout you can do anywhere — while still providing many of the benefits of traditional Pilates.

REFORMER WORKOUTS
- uses a spring-based machine to add resistance.Highly effective because the machine's design adds resistance, support, and versatility, allowing for a deeper, more targeted workout.
