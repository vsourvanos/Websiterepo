Q&A Hub

Your questions answered — everything about classes, membership, and our facilities.

1

What should I wear to classes?

Comfortable athletic wear is recommended. Padded cycling shorts or moisture-wicking tops help, and proper shoes are required.

2

Do I need to book classes in advance?

Yes, booking guarantees your spot. You can reserve via our app or at the front desk.

3

Can I bring a guest?

Guests are welcome but must be registered and accompanied by a member. Guest passes are available for a fee.

4

What if I need to cancel a class?

Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

5

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, certified trainers offer personal sessions. Booking in advance is recommended due to limited slots.

6

What is the refund policy?

Membership fees are non-refundable. Exceptions are detailed in the membership agreement.

7

Can I bring my own equipment?

Personal items are allowed if approved by staff. Standard equipment is provided for all classes.

8

Are lockers and showers available?

Yes, clean locker rooms with showers and changing areas are available for all members.

9

Is the gym suitable for beginners?

Yes! Classes are designed for all fitness levels, and instructors offer modifications so beginners feel comfortable and supported.

10

How intense are the workouts?

Intensity varies by class type, but you control your resistance and pace, making it adaptable to everyone.

11

Is parking available?

Yes, free parking is available for members and guests.

12

Are there age restrictions?

Members must meet minimum age requirements. Youth memberships are available with guardian consent.

13

Do you have a lost and found?

Yes, lost items are kept at the front desk for a limited time.

14

Do you offer student or family discounts?

Yes, discounted plans are available for students, families, and corporate members.

15

Do you have Wi-Fi in the facility?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the gym.
