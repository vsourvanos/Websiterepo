Rules & Policies

Safety, respect, and consistency keep our gym strong.

Gym Rules & Policies

All members must have a valid membership card or app for entry. Guests must be registered and approved in advance. Access is limited to operating hours and designated areas only.

  • Membership cards are personal and non-transferable.
  • Lost cards must be reported immediately; replacements may incur a fee.
  • Members must comply with age restrictions for certain areas or classes.
  • Guests must sign a waiver and follow all gym rules.

Your health and safety are our top priority. Always warm up and cool down properly, follow instructor guidance, and use equipment responsibly.

  • Wear appropriate footwear and clothing for activities.
  • Hydrate regularly, especially during high-intensity workouts.
  • Inform staff of injuries or health conditions prior to class.
  • Stop exercising immediately if you experience dizziness, chest pain, or severe fatigue.
  • Follow posted safety instructions on all machines and in all areas.

Maintain a positive, respectful environment for everyone. Disruptive, aggressive, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

  • Respect instructors, staff, and fellow members at all times.
  • No harassment, bullying, or offensive language.
  • Keep personal music devices at reasonable volume or use headphones.
  • Clean up after yourself; leave shared spaces tidy.

Proper use of gym equipment ensures safety and longevity. Treat all equipment as if it were your own.

  • Wipe down machines, bikes, and mats after use.
  • Return weights, accessories, and other items to their designated spots.
  • Do not reserve equipment for others or leave personal belongings on machines.
  • Report broken or malfunctioning equipment immediately to staff.

Arrive on time and follow instructor guidance to ensure a smooth, productive class experience for all participants.

  • Arrive at least 10 minutes early; late arrivals may not be admitted.
  • Reservations required for all group classes.
  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance to avoid penalties.
  • Follow all instructions for equipment setup and use during class.
  • Children under supervision only in designated areas; no drop-offs in classes.

Locker rooms and showers are shared spaces. Keep them clean and safe for everyone.

  • Lock personal items securely; gym is not responsible for lost items.
  • Shower before and after workouts to maintain hygiene.
  • Dispose of trash properly; do not leave towels or clothing on the floor.
  • Follow all posted signs regarding locker room conduct and safety.

Membership fees and policies are clearly defined in the membership agreement. Familiarize yourself with terms to avoid misunderstandings.

  • Memberships are non-transferable and non-refundable except as stated in the agreement.
  • Members must notify staff of any changes in contact information.
  • Policies and fees may be updated; members will be notified at least 30 days in advance.
  • Failure to follow gym rules may result in suspension or termination of membership.

Helpful tips for a smooth and enjoyable gym experience:

  • Always bring a water bottle, towel, and proper attire to all sessions.
  • Label personal belongings to prevent mix-ups or loss.
  • Follow safety signs and instructions posted around the gym.
  • Ask staff for help if unsure about equipment, exercises, or class techniques.
  • Respect peak hour guidelines; share space and equipment with others.
  • Keep phone calls brief and maintain a professional gym environment.
