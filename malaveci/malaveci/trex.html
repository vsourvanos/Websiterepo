WHAT IS T-REX?

T-Rex is a high-intensity power training system focused on maximal strength, explosive output, and total-body resilience. Every session is structured around heavy compound lifts, athletic movement patterns, and controlled overload.

This program is engineered for individuals who demand visible performance improvements, not aesthetic workouts. Every rep has intent. Every session has purpose.

01

STRENGTH PHASE
